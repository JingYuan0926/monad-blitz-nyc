## MrBeast

| Field | Info |
|---|---|
| **Real name** | James Stephen "Jimmy" Donaldson |
| **Born** | May 7, 1998 — Wichita, Kansas, USA |
| **Based in** | Greenville, North Carolina, USA |
| **Known for** | Biggest YouTuber in the world; large-scale challenge & giveaway videos |
| **YouTube** | @MrBeast — 350M+ subscribers (most-subscribed individual channel ever) |
| **Other channels** | MrBeast Gaming, Beast Reacts, Beast Philanthropy, MrBeast 2 |

### Career highlights
- Started YouTube in 2012 at age 13; broke out in 2017 with the "counting to 100,000" video.
- Pioneered the high-budget challenge format: "Last To Leave Circle Wins $500,000", "$456,000 Squid Game in Real Life", "I Spent 50 Hours Buried Alive".
- **Beast Games** (Amazon Prime Video, 2024–) — largest reality competition show ever produced, $5M grand prize.
- Reinvests most video revenue back into production — budgets often exceed $3–4M per video.

### Businesses & philanthropy
- **Feastables** — chocolate/snack brand sold in major retailers worldwide.
- **MrBeast Burger** — virtual restaurant brand (launched 2020).
- **Beast Philanthropy** — dedicated channel funding food banks; 100% of its revenue goes to charity.
- **#TeamTrees** (20M trees with Mark Rober) and **#TeamSeas** (30M lbs of ocean trash) — viral fundraising campaigns.
- "1,000 Blind People See For The First Time" — funded cataract surgeries worldwide.

### Style / persona
- Fast-paced editing, big numbers in titles, generosity-as-content.
- Famous for obsessive focus on thumbnails, retention, and "the first 10 seconds".

## Kevin Hart

| Field | Info |
|---|---|
| **Full name** | Kevin Darnell Hart |
| **Born** | July 6, 1979 — Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA |
| **Known for** | Stand-up comedy, blockbuster comedies, prolific social media presence |
| **Height** | 5'2" (157 cm) — a running joke in his own material |
| **Social reach** | 175M+ on Instagram; massive YouTube (LOL Network) presence |

### Stand-up career
- Came up through Philadelphia/New York comedy clubs in the late 1990s.
- Breakout specials: *I'm a Grown Little Man* (2009), *Seriously Funny* (2010), *Laugh at My Pain* (2011), *Let Me Explain* (2013).
- *What Now?* (2016) — performed to 53,000 people at Lincoln Financial Field, one of the largest stand-up shows ever.
- Among the highest-grossing touring comedians in history.

### Film & TV
- *Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle* / *The Next Level* (with Dwayne Johnson)
- *Ride Along* 1–2, *Central Intelligence*, *Night School*, *The Upside*
- Voice of Snowball in *The Secret Life of Pets*
- *Die Hart* (Emmy-winning streaming series), *Lift* (Netflix)
- Mark Twain Prize for American Humor recipient (2024)

### Businesses
- **HartBeat** — his media/production company (valued ~$650M after 2022 investment).
- **LOL! Network** (Laugh Out Loud) — comedy streaming/digital network.
- **Gran Coramino** — tequila brand with extensive charitable giving.
- Investor/pitchman: Fabletics Men, Chase, DraftKings, plant-based restaurant Hart House.

### Style / persona
- Self-deprecating, high-energy storytelling about family, fear, and his height.
- Famously disciplined: 4 a.m. workouts, "Everybody Wants To Be Famous, Nobody Wants To Put The Work In."
